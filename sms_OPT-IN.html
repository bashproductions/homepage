Proof Of Consent Disclaimer

Proof of Customer Consent for SMS Messaging

Bash Productions LLC's primary buisiness is booking entertainers for various events. The goal of this optional SMS service is to provide text-based booking confirmations and weekly schedule reminders; opting into this additional service is not required to continue working with Bash Productions LLC. This optional SMS service will not be used for sending marketing messages or promotions.

In order to provide this optional service, Bash Productions LLC will collect the following information from interested & consenting team-members: name, phone number, timestamps & consent confirmation logs. The following procedures are in place to ensure that all messaging is compliant with industry standards and regulations:

1. Opt-In Process:

  • Team-members provide their express written consent by completing the online signup form on this webpage: (bashproductionsllc.com/sms_OPT-IN.html). The form includes a clear description of the types of messages they will receive, such as event booking & schedule reminders.
  • During the signup process, team-members are required to check the Opt-In checkbox confirming that they are providing their express written consent to receive SMS messages from Bash Productions LLC and are aware that message and data rates may apply.
  • Alternatively, team-members may provide consent by texting "JOIN" to our toll-free number, explicitly confirming their consent to receive communications from us.
  • Additionally, as proof of confirmation of consent, a follow-up message will be sent asking them to confirm their consent by replying "YES".

2. Consent Documentation:

  • All opt-in records, including the team-member's name, phone number, timestamp, and source of consent, are securely stored in our database for reference and compliance purposes.
  • We maintain these records for as long as the team-member remains subscribed, and we keep logs for regulatory review as needed.

3. Opt-Out Mechanism:

  • Team-members can opt out of receiving messages at any time by replying with "STOP." Our system automatically processes opt-out requests and ensures that no further messages are sent to the team-member after receiving such a request.
  • The Weekly Schedule reminder message we send includes instructions for opting out, e.g.: "Reply STOP to opt out of receiving these messages."

4. Frequency and Message Content:

  • Messages are sent to team-members based on the specific purpose for which they opted-in: booking confirmations & weekly schedule reminders. Frequency will vary based on the number of engagements on which the team-member has been booked, but team-members will not receive more messages than necessary to provide adequate scheduling reminders and confirmations for the events on which they've been booked.
  • All messages are relevant to the services requested by the team-member and are not used for unrelated marketing or promotional purposes.

Compliance and Transparency

  • We comply with all applicable carrier regulations, including those governing toll-free messaging, and ensure our practices align with both Twilio's policies and industry standards.
  • We do not send unsolicited messages, and we only communicate with team-members who have provided documented proof of their explicit consent.
