Proof Of Consent Disclaimer

Proof of Customer Consent for SMS Messaging

Bash Productions LLC collects and stores explicit consent from all team-members before sending SMS or MMS communications. The following procedures are in place to ensure that all messaging is compliant with industry standards and regulations:

1. Opt-In Process:

  • Team-members provide their consent by completing the online signup form on this webpage:
    (bashproductionsllc.com/
    sms_opt_in.html). The form includes a clear description of the types of messages they will receive, such as event booking & schedule reminders.
  • During the signup process, team-members are required to check the Opt-In checkbox with the following disclaimer: "I agree to receive SMS notifications from Bash Productions LLC about event bookings & schedule reminders. Message and data rates may apply."
  • Alternatively, team-members may provide consent by texting "JOIN" to our toll-free number, explicitly confirming their desire to receive communications from us.

2. Consent Documentation:

  • All opt-in records, including the team-member's name, phone number, timestamp, and source of consent, are securely stored in our database for reference and compliance purposes.
  • We maintain these records for as long as the team-member remains subscribed, and we keep logs for regulatory review as needed.

3. Opt-Out Mechanism:

  • Team-members can opt out of receiving messages at any time by replying with "STOP." Our system automatically processes opt-out requests and ensures that no further messages are sent to the team-member after receiving such a request.
  • Every message we send includes instructions for opting out, e.g.: "Reply STOP to opt out of receiving these messages".

4. Frequency and Message Content:

  • Messages are sent to team-members based on the specific purpose for which they opted in. Frequency may vary depending on the selected use case and/or the number of engagements the team-member has been booked on, but team-members will not receive any more messages than is necessary to provide adequate scheduling reminders for the events on which they've been booked (unless explicitly stated otherwise during opt-in).
  • All messages are relevant to the services requested by the team-member and are not used for unrelated marketing purposes.

Compliance and Transparency

  • We comply with all applicable carrier regulations, including those governing toll-free messaging, and ensure that our practices align with both Twilio's policies and industry standards.
  • We do not send unsolicited messages, and we only communicate with team-members who have provided documented proof of their consent.

Contact Information

  • For any questions regarding our SMS policies, or to request further information, please contact Gordon Bash at bashproductionsllc@gmail.com.
